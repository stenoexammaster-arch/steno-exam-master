Quick FAQs

  • Do you provide phone or WhatsApp support?

    At the moment, we provide support only through email at stenoexammaster@gmail.com. This helps us keep your queries organized and provide accurate written guidance.

  • What details should I include for typing test issues?

    Please mention the test type (Hindi/English), your approximate speed, browser (Chrome/Edge, etc.), device (mobile/desktop), and the exact problem (for example: result mismatch, timer issue, keyboard problem, or page not loading).

  • Can I ask for preparation guidance and book suggestions?

    Yes. You can ask for a practice strategy, exam pattern clarification, and book or course suggestions for major central and state-level steno and typing exams.
